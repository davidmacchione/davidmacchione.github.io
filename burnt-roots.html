“Burnt Roots” becomes an exploration of roots and identity inspired by the sounds and impressions of the Italian countryside and its people.

David Macchione's debut recording “Burnt Roots - story of an olive tree” tackles the xylella fastidiosa outbreak that since October of 2013 has started to decimate the olive tree population in his home region of Puglia, in Southern Italy.

10 years later, science has not yet been able to find a cure for the disease, and the only solution seems to be violent eradication. But the further you look into this issue the more questions start arising- Has intensive farming and an indiscriminate use of herbicides brought us here?

Uncovering the tale of a history of struggle between local farmers and the central government, and of modern science versus ancient wisdom, “Burnt Roots” becomes an exploration of roots and identity inspired by the sounds and impressions of the Italian countryside and its people.

Artist Bio

Based between New York and Amsterdam, David Macchione’s solid approach to bass playing and developed musical sensibility make him a sought-after player for various corners of contemporary jazz and original music.

He is a graduate of the Conservatorium van Amsterdam, and the Berklee Global Jazz Institute Master in Global Jazz program, where he studied with Linda May Han Oh, Danilo Perez, Jason Palmer and Kris Davis.

Band Bio

The David Macchione Quintet was founded in 2019 at the Conservatorium van Amsterdam, with the objective of bringing together exciting up and coming voices of the European and global Jazz scene.

Revolving around the leader and bass player, this fluid ensemble performs an ever evolving original repertoire that dares and invites the group to explore creative new ways of playing. “Burnt Roots” is its debut recording, tackling the xylella outbreak in Southern Italy centering themes of ecology, class politics and resilience.

The group has performed at the renowned Jamboree in Barcelona, and various other venues in Italy, Spain, The Netherlands and Boston.
